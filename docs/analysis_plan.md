\# Kerala Elections – Initial Analysis Plan



\## Objective

To analyze the \*\*Kerala Legislative Assembly Elections\*\* for the years \*\*2011, 2016, and 2021\*\*, using data science methods to extract trends, patterns, and predictive signals from the results.



---



\## What Patterns Will We Explore?

\- Vote share swings between years (e.g., how CPI(M)'s vote changed from 2016 to 2021)

\- Alliance wins/losses (LDF vs UDF vs NDA)

\- Turnout trends by year and district

\- Victory margins – close vs landslide wins

\- Constituency-level behavior (swing seats vs strongholds)



---



\## Years of Interest

\- 2011

\- 2016

\- 2021  

\*(+ any available elections)\*



---



\## Metrics to Compute

\- Vote share (%) per party/alliance

\- Margin of victory (number and %)

\- Voter turnout (%)

\- Total seats won by each party/alliance

\- Number of contests per candidate (if doing career tracking)



---



\## Tools \& Libraries
